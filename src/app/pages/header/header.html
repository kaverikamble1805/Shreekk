Know The Real History of
Shree Shakti Mandir, Leicester UK

Shree Shakti Mandir UK is a sacred center of devotion, spirituality, and Hindu culture where devotees come together to worship Maa Shakti and seek divine blessings. The temple has become a symbol of faith and unity for the Hindu community across the United Kingdom, preserving the rich traditions of Sanatan Dharma for future generations.

  • Daily Aarti & Spiritual Prayer Ceremonies
  • Grand Celebration of Navratri & Diwali Festivals
  • Community Center & Service
  • Charity Programs
  • Teaching Hindu Culture & Spiritual Values
  • Peaceful Place for Meditation & Devotion

Established in 1975
